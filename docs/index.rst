Mopidy-InternetArchive
========================================================================

Mopidy-InternetArchive is a Mopidy_ extension for playing music and
audio from the `Internet Archive`_.

This extension lets you search for and stream music from the vast
collection of free live recordings of the Live Music Archive, a.k.a
the etree_ collection, but also lets you access all other kinds of
free/open source audio.
